Caption: For those new to the idea of sketching or out of the habit, jumping straight into it as part of the design process may be intimidating and might yield unproductive results. But I do believe it's worthwhile to get comfortable with pen and paper. Drawing out any ideas, outside one's design process—even an idiosyncratic invention—can help to build the association between sketching and idea creation.

----

Alt: "Start drawing outside of the design process. When you get comfortable with pen and paper, it won't be a barrier when brainstorming." With a silly invention idea for ultra-nerds like myself—a clip to hold a pencil on the end of your glasses' arm, so you can have one behind your ear despite near-sightedness.
